Guide on using app

Here we learn how app is structured and how you can get what you want

Learn three buttons on homepage and how they can help you

Green life

The button takes you to information on natural life and how non biodegradable materials can harm it.

Live safer

It takes you to page which contains information about recycling and teach people how they can recycle by themselves.

Take part

It contains names and contacts about some recycling industries, enabling people to get connected to them by just clicking on their numbers and text to them and it also contain an add button that will enable other recycling industries to add their data also and get into the app.

Get updated

This button enable people using the app to directly connect to an environmental protection website, this will allow people to stay updated about it.
